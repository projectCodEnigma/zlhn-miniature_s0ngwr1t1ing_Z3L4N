About

The Craft
Behind the Numbers.

Zelhan is a Cebuano composer and producer based in Cebu, Philippines. His compositions have reached 68M+ combined streams — not through performance alone, but because the songwriting itself pulls audiences in across borders and languages.

His breakout composition "Kaon Na Ta (Let's Eat)" became a phenomenon with 59.5M+ Spotify streams. Heartfelt works including "Pauli Na Ko," "Langga," and "Ikaw Ra" established Zelhan as a distinctive voice in Cebuano pop and OPM.

Today, Zelhan is repositioning as a composer-for-hire and co-writer — available for vocalists, artists, brands, and sync supervisors who want a proven melodic mind on their project.
