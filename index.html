About

Visayan Stories.
Global Ears.

Zelhan is a Cebuano singer-songwriter blending the warmth of Visayan storytelling with contemporary alternative pop. He creates music that travels — from intimate ballads sung in Bisaya to viral hooks that resonate far beyond the islands.

His breakout single "Ikaw Ra" introduced his modern Bisaya sound. It was followed by heartfelt releases including "Pauli Na Ko", "Langga," and the phenomenon that became "Kaon Na Ta (Let's Eat)" — now past 1.28 million streams.

Today, Zelhan writes custom songs for weddings, brands, and life's defining moments — bringing Cebuano culture to global ears, one story at a time.
